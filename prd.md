## 🔍 SEO REQUIREMENTS (Critical — implement ALL of these)

### On-Page SEO
- **Title Tags:** Unique, keyword-rich for every page (max 60 chars)
  - Home: `"Nektra Energy Solutions | Exide Industrial Battery Dealer Hyderabad"`
  - Products: `"Industrial Forklift Batteries | Exide Motive Power | Nektra Energy"`
  - Industries: `"Battery Solutions for [Industry] | Nektra Energy Hyderabad"`
- **Meta Descriptions:** Unique, compelling, with CTA (max 155 chars) for every page
- **H1 Tags:** One per page, keyword-optimized
- **Header Hierarchy:** Proper H1 > H2 > H3 structure on every page
- **Image Alt Tags:** Descriptive, keyword-rich alt text on every image
- **Internal Linking:** Cross-link products ↔ industries ↔ services extensively
- **URL Structure:** Clean, lowercase, hyphenated slugs

### Technical SEO
- **Next.js SSR/SSG** — Pre-rendered pages for fast crawling
- **Sitemap:** Auto-generated `/sitemap.xml` with all pages
- **Robots.txt:** Proper configuration at `/robots.txt`
- **Canonical URLs:** Self-referencing canonicals on every page
- **Open Graph Tags:** Title, description, image for every page (social sharing)
- **Twitter Card Tags:** Summary large image cards
- **Structured Data / Schema Markup (JSON-LD):**
  - `Organization` — company name, address, phone, logo, area served
  - `LocalBusiness` — full NAP (Name, Address, Phone), opening hours, geo coordinates
  - `Product` — for each battery product with specs
  - `Service` — for each service offered
  - `BreadcrumbList` — on every page
  - `FAQPage` — on FAQ page
  - `Article` — on blog posts
  - `WebSite` — with SearchAction for sitelinks search box
- **Performance:**
  - Lazy loading for all images
  - Next.js Image optimization
  - Font preloading
  - Minimal JS bundle
  - Target: 90+ Lighthouse scores
- **Mobile-First:** Fully responsive, touch-friendly, fast on 3G
- **Core Web Vitals:** Optimize LCP, FID, CLS

### Content SEO Strategy
- Every industry page targets: `"[industry] forklift battery Hyderabad"`, `"[industry] material handling battery India"`
- Every product page targets: `"exide [product type] battery dealer"`, `"[product type] traction battery Hyderabad"`
- Blog targets long-tail keywords: `"how to maintain forklift battery"`, `"best battery for warehouse forklift India"`
- FAQ targets "People Also Ask" queries
- Use semantic HTML throughout (`<article>`, `<section>`, `<nav>`, `<aside>`, `<main>`)
- Breadcrumb navigation on every page

### Local SEO
- NAP consistency across all pages (Name, Address, Phone)
- Embed Google Maps on contact page
- Area-specific content: "Serving Hyderabad, Secunderabad, Chandanagar, Kukatpally, Miyapur, Gachibowli, HITEC City, Medchal, Shamshabad, and all of Telangana"
- Schema LocalBusiness with geo coordinates (17.4933° N, 78.3222° E approximate for Chandanagar)
